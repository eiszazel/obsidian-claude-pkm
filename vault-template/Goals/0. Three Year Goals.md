# Three Year Goals (2026-2029)

## Vision Statement

**Process Management / Platform Owner** (ServiceNow orbit), known internally as the person who:
- Turns messy work into **clean workflows**
- Ships **agentic automations** that actually reduce toil
- Can translate between ops, compliance, engineering, and leadership

**Target Comp:** $150k–$200k+ with real ownership and reputation.

---

## Key Life Areas

### Career & Professional Development
- [ ] Reach **Process Manager / Platform Owner** role
- [ ] Master **agentic automation + LLM tooling** for enterprise workflows
- [ ] Build portfolio of **internal process wins** that leadership references
- [ ] Develop **token engineering fundamentals** + basic risk/statistics fluency
- [ ] Become go-to for **ServiceNow + automation** in the organization

### Health & Wellness
- [ ] **Aesthetic focus:** broader shoulders/arms, improved posture, consistent "looksmaxxing"
- [ ] **Skin/hair:** locked routines—face, body, dandruff managed, clean haircut cadence
- [ ] **Energy:** stable mornings, no crash cycles, repeatable diet
- [ ] **Habit default:** gym is automatic, not motivation-based

### Relationships & Family
- [ ] **Partner:** aligned on lifestyle, money, long-term location strategy—"team mode"
- [ ] **Friends/community:** smaller, higher-trust circle; intentional hangs; fewer drains
- [ ] **Network:** more high-agency people (builders, operators, investors), both online + IRL

### Personal Growth & Learning
- [ ] **π-shaped skills:** automation/IT ops + product/process + finance/tokenization literacy
- [ ] **Communication:** concise, executive-friendly writing + calm authority in meetings
- [ ] **Leadership traits:** ownership, follow-through, boundary-setting, consistency
- [ ] **Technical growth:** agents/LLM tooling, workflow design, token engineering

### Financial Security
- [ ] **Freedom + liquidity:** money accessible when markets are ugly
- [ ] **Multiple income engines:** not just "price go up," but cashflow mechanisms you control
- [ ] **Compounding with intent:** aggressive growth with defensive structure
- [ ] **Optionality:** ability to relocate, take risk, choose projects without panic

### Creativity & Fun
- [ ] Building **code/systems** (dashboards, automations, bots, PWAs)
- [ ] **Crypto/DeFi tinkering** as both hobby + strategy
- [ ] **Music experiments** (AI/Suno output as "creative texture")
- [ ] Style/aesthetics upgrades (capsule wardrobe, grooming)
- [ ] Travel experiences (Mexico City-style weekends)
- [ ] Return to fitness hobbies (rock climbing, etc.)

---

## Success Metrics

### Year 1 (2026) - Foundation
- ITIL Foundation + PSM certifications earned
- One visible automation/process win shipped at work
- Gym + skincare routines locked and visible results
- Weekly review + goal tracking running consistently

### Year 2 (2027) - Acceleration
- Promoted to Process Manager or equivalent role
- Multiple automation wins in portfolio
- Income engines beyond salary generating cash flow
- Network of high-agency builders established

### Year 3 (2028-2029) - Compound
- Platform Owner role with $150k+ comp
- Known externally for workflow/automation expertise
- Antifragile financial position with multiple income streams
- Location flexibility achieved

## Reflection Questions

1. Am I building **systems** or just doing tasks?
2. What's generating **compound returns** vs one-off effort?

4. Who in my network is raising my standards?
5. What would "future me" cut from today's priorities?
